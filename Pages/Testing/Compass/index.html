Berwick College 2020 European Study Tour Information Evening
Dear Parents and Guardians,
As part of the enrichment of student opportunities at Berwick College we are pleased to invite students enrolled in Years 9, 10 and 11 in 2020 to participate in a European History and Cultural Experience.
 
The project is a wonderful and valuable opportunity for students to develop their independence experience the rich history and culture of Europe along with an opportunity to develop awareness of art, music and tourism. The course aligns to studies in History, Geography, English, Art and Literature however is open to all students wishing to experience a unique and varied travel opportunity.
Students will travel to Poland, Belgium, France and England and will visit numerous cultural and historic sights, along with some tourist highlights such as Euro Disney and Harry Potter World. (Full Itinerary attached).
An information session will be held on Tuesday 25th of June in the Main Staff Room at Berwick College starting at 6pm for all interested students and families. This is the perfect opportunity to gain further information about this amazing experience.
The camp will run from Wednesday 2nd September, 2020 to Sunday 20th September, 2020 (exact travel dates tbc) and cost will include transport, some meals and accommodation.
The total cost of the camp is approximately $6779.00 (TBC as final costs are not yet available due to the long time frame for payments. This is intentional to allow parents time to establish a payment plan).
A Deposit of $300 is required to secure your child’s place by Friday 20th September 2019.  To ensure your payment is received by the 20th September please allow 5 business days for any Compass, BPay, QKR payments. Should there be fewer than 5 days please make payment directly at our front office or by phone.
 
If you are interested in finding out more, please contact Mrs Michelle Lewis via email lewis.michelle.n@edumail.vic.gov.au
 
Kind regards,
Mrs Michelle Lewis                                                                                                                                                                                                                         
Flynn  Student Learning Leader/ Camps and Activities Co-ordinator
